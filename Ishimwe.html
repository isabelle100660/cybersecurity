CYBER THREATS TRAINING

used to equip employees with the practical knowledge and
understanding of the digital landscape. Identifying the various security threats
that could occur when working with different types of technology
ex computers, laptops, mobiles, tablets, printers networks and cloud services.

Typically, cybersecurity training teaches response procedures
for addressing and managing risk to computer systems.
Participants can learn how to identify threats like cyber attacks, data
hacks and phishing activities, how to assess risk levels,
how to report incidents, and how they can be addressed.
